Merge Sort is a complex sort algorithm, but also is efficient

To execute it: 
1° Get an array
2° Divide it until you can not do it anymore
3° Conquer it:
    3° a. Sort each subarray
    3° b. Merge each sorted subarray

example:
6,1,4,8,9,3,5,2

6,1,4,8   9,3,5,2

6,1   4,8   9,3   5,2

6  1  4  8  9  3  5  2

1,6  4,8  9,3  2,5

1,4,6,8   2,3,5,9

1,2,3,4,5,6,8,9
